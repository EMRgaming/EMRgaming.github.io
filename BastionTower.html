Turret System

The turret system was something I decided I wanted to take on myself. I worked torwards trying to have a fun exciting way to have the turrets be useful to the player and a way to upgrade the turrets so that the player has assistance taking on the enemies. I started with the basic turret and worked on tracking enemies and getting the turret to aim torwards and shooting at said enemy.





Next I moved on to being able to buy and place a turret where the player would like. I started with making a menu that would allow the player to select the turret to purchase. Then I added a placeholder turret to show the player where the turret would be placed. This was a fun and interesting process trying to figure out how to have the turrets spawn and be palced correctly according to the player.





I also worked on designing most of the menus for our game.
