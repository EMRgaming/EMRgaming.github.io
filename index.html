I went back to school at Full Sail University and wasn't really sure my end goals but I knew I wanted to write code. I started working torwards a bacholers degree in Video Game development and eventually decided to move to Computer Science. If for some reason I'm not playing games or working I'm probably fishing or out at the golf course.
